NASA Meteorite Landings

Welcome! This site will tell you for each country what types of meteorites have hit it, the count of meteorites, and the mean mass of those meteorites.

If one were to take interest in visiting meteor sites, then this app is useful for that person to find out what country he/she could visit to get exposed to the biggest variety of meteorites.
